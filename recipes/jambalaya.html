Jambalaya

Description:

Jambalaya is a flavorful dish traditional in Cajun cooking.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of long grain rice
  • 3 cups of chicken broth
  • 1 cup of water
  • 4 andouille sausage links
  • 1 lb of ham (cubed)
  • 1 lb of shrimp (peeled)
  • 1 red bell pepper (diced)
  • 1 yellow or green bell pepper
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 1-2 jalapenos (diced)
  • 3 stalks of celery (diced)
  • green onion for garnish on top
  • old bay seasoning
  • cajun seasoning
  • 5 cloves of garlic
